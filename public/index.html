How to Link Device

  1. Open WhatsApp on your primary phone.
  2. Go to Settings > Linked Devices.
  3. Tap Link a Device and select Link with phone number.
  4. Enter the pairing code generated above in the provided field.
  5. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the linking process.
