Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload a profile picture?

Simply drag and drop your image into the upload zone or click to browse. We support JPG and PNG formats up to 5MB.

Is my data secure?

Yes! All data is encrypted and automatically deleted after your profile update is complete. We don't store any personal information.

What happens if I get an error?

If you encounter any errors, try refreshing the page and uploading again. Make sure your image meets the format and size requirements.
